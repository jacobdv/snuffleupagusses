High Speed Internet Analysis

The following visualizations explore the relationship between high-speed internet access and community measures such as median income, population, and education level. The folowing visualizations utilize internet speed data from the FCC and demographic information from the US Census. High speed internet was defined as download speeds of 25 megabytes per second or greater, which is the FCC limit for broadband internet.
